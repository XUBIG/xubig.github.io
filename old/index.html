about me

I am a researcher working at the intersection of geospatial data science, remote sensing, and artificial intelligence. My work focuses on landslide detection and monitoring, disaster information extraction from social media, and the fusion of multi-source geospatial data (satellite imagery, DEM, rainfall, landcover, susceptibility maps) to improve situational awareness during natural hazards.

Broadly, I am interested in how multi-modal data (text, images, and geospatial layers) can be combined to infer when and where landslides occur and how their impacts evolve in space and time.
