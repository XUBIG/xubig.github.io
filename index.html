about me

I conduct research at the intersection of geospatial data science, remote sensing, and AI. My focus is on landslide detection, disaster information extraction from social media, and multi-source data fusion.

I am exploring how multi-modal data (text/images/geospatial layers) can be leveraged to infer the precise timing and location of landslides.
